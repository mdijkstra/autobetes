  1. Logbook

    The logbook lists the consumptions and events that you have recorded so far. Here you can edit them.

  1. Advices

    Currently, this app is not approved to come up with advices directly for its users. During your consult, you may ask your physician to unlock our advices.

  1. Connect to moves (only once!)

    Please press this button to connect the Autobetes app to the 'Moves app'. We make use of that app to automatically track your physical activities for later analysis.

  2. Force synchronization

    Your data should be automatically uploaded to our server for analysis. If this does not happen, please press this button.

  3. Sensor plot

    Expert option: only for users with a Raspberry Pi.

  4. Feedback

    We are happy to receive your feedback, questions and suggestions! We are happy to help you!

  5. Logout

    Here you can log out from your account. Only use if needed.
